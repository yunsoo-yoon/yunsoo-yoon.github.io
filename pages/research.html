Self‑powered Wearable Haptics

Centimeter‑scale permanent‑magnet generator with Halbach array harvesting pneumatic gait energy to power a microcontroller for haptic sensing/actuation.

  • Designed & optimized generator using Faraday‑based model; explored Halbach topologies.
  • Targeting on‑body integration with compliant transmission and conductive textiles.
